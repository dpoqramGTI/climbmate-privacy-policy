Request to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your ClimbMate account and associated personal data, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to climbmatesoporte@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account".
  2. In the email, please provide your registered email address and any other identifying information (e.g., name, DNI).

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete the following data:

  • Your name and surname
  • Your email address
  • Your date of birth
  • Your phone number
  • Your DNI

Retention of Data: We will retain any data required by law (such as financial transaction records) for a specified retention period before deletion.

Response Time

We will process your account deletion request within 30 days.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding the deletion process, feel free to contact us at climbmatesoporte@gmail.com.
